Every particle located at least `Nstdx`, `Nstdy` or `Nstdz` standard deviations away from the average *x-*, *y-* or *z-*coordinate of the bunch is removed.

This element is intended to ‘clean up’ the simulation by removing all outliers, all the particles located too far from the center of the bunch. This can be used to solve the following problems: • Outliers can affect the GDFA data analysis routines and cause unrealistic values for bunch length, energy spread etc. • Outliers can significantly increase the simulation time for a variety of reasons.
