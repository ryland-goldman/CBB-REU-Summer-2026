### 5.5 Absorber geometry and Dimensions

The absorber is cylindrically symmetric around the beam axis. The beampipe’s radii come from the
flange specifications in the files that define the profiles of the absWindow and the safetyWindow; the
beampipe’s length is extended to the same length as the safetyWindow-s on the beam axis. The
safetyDistance is between the inner faces of the two windows on the beam axis. If safetyWindow is
omitted, all references to it are changed to absWindow, and there are no volumes of safetyMaterial.
