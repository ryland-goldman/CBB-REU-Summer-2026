# 8.1 Internal Coordinates of Particle

The coordinates of particle used inside the IMPACT-T are dimensionless variables:

```
x/dz,  px/mc,  y/dz,  py/mc,  z/dz,  pz/mc
```

where the scale length `dz = c*dt` with `dt` being the initial time step size, the momentum `pk = γβk` for `k = x, y, z`, `βk = vk/c`, `vk` is the velocity in each direction and `c` is the speed of light.
